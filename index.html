About Ms.Cook

Located in the Toronto Ontario, founded by Sediqa Hadid in 2022. Ms. cook serves modern cuisine with a palpable Ontario sensibility. From morning to night, Chef Ms.Lynn Crawford offers tastes of contemporary Afghan, Italian and London foods just steps from the leafy, lively sanctuary of CN tower. A 90-seat gastronomic gem, the restaurant comprises three intimate dining rooms, a dedicated billiards room and each individual space exuding its own personality.

Chef Lynn Crawford

Lynn Crawford was born July 18, 1964 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. She trained at George Brown College in Toronto. She apprenticed under Alice Waters. She was formerly the executive chef at the Four Seasons in Toronto and the former executive chef of the Four Seasons in New York. From 2010 until 2021 she co-founded and co-owned alongside Lora Kirk, the table dhôte restaurant Ruby Watchoo in Toronto.
